# Music-and-Song-Analysis-for-Music-Streaming-Companies

As a consulting firm in the field of data analysis, we were able to secure a project with a music streaming service, which is a relatively new entrant in the market. They are currently our client, and they want to know where they need to improve in order to compete in the market and also they want to improve their recommendation system and further. They need to know how people listen to music in the music industry. They have a large dataset with 20 features, and they have some specific requests for us.

That music streaming company wants to know some answers from that dataset. Those are that we need to answer to complete our task for that music streaming company:

What audio features are most correlated with song popularity?

Do energetic songs tend to be more popular?

Please show the relationship between danceability and popularity

Which genres have the highest average tempo?

How does song duration affect popularity across different duration ranges?

How do audio features differ across genres?

Can we identify clusters of songs based on their audio features?
